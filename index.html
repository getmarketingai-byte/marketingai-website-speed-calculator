Speed Optimisation Tips

  • Target under 3 seconds: Google says 53% of users leave a site that takes >3s to load.
  • Optimise images: Compress and use modern formats (WebP).
  • Enable caching: Browser caching reduces repeat load times.
  • Use a CDN: Content Delivery Networks serve files from closer locations.
